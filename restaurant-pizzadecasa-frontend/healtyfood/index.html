MENU

MARGHERITA

In June 1889 pizzaiolo Raffaele Esposito invented the "Pizza Margherita" in honor of the Queen of Italy, Margherita di Savoia, and the unification of Italy. Its ingredients represent the colors of the flag. Ingredients: house tomato sauce, mozzarella, tomato, basil, olive oil and oregano.

PUGLIESE

Named after the Italian region of Puglia, this pizza mixes the subtle flavor of peppers and tomatoes with the crunchiness of the dough. Some consider it the perfect vegetarian pizza. Ingredients: house tomato sauce, mozzarella, tomato, red pepper, white cheese, parsley, oil and oregano.

CALABRESA

The Brazilian adaptation of the classic Italian pizza, Salame piccante. The simplicity and delicious taste make this pizza everyone's favorite. Ingredients: house tomato sauce, mozzarella, pepperoni sausage, onion (optional) and olive oil

CAPRICCIOSA

Our adaptation of the famous Italian pizza combines the taste of mushroom and olive with the strong flavor of sirloin. Capricciosa is mouth-watering just thinking about it! Ingredients: house tomato sauce, mozzarella, loin, mushrooms, olives, oil and oregano.

AMOR DE ALHO

The garlicky taste that helped Romanians ward off vampires for thousands of years is now in Brazil! Our love for garlic is great and we want to share it with the world. Ingredients: white sauce with garlic, parmesan, mozzarella, oil and oregano.
